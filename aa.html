Rich Dad Poor Dad

A book by Robert Kiyosaki explaining financial education and the difference between rich and poor mindsets.

The Lean Startup

A book by Eric Ries about how startups can use lean principles to succeed with less risk.

Thinking, Fast and Slow

A psychology book by Daniel Kahneman that explores two modes of thinking: fast (intuitive) and slow (logical).
