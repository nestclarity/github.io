Rich Dad Poor Dad

Author: Robert Kiyosaki

Summary: Rich Dad Poor Dad* explores the difference between the financial mindset of the rich and the poor.
Kiyosaki tells the story of his two father figuresâ€”his biological "Poor Dad," who believed in traditional education and job security, and his "Rich Dad,"

The book highlights how wealth comes from acquiring assets, such as businesses and real estate, rather than liabilities that drain money. Kiyosaki introduces concepts like "making money work for you," financial intelligence, and escaping the "rat race." He argues that schools fail to teach real financial literacy, leaving people trapped in a cycle of working for money instead of achieving financial independence. Through practical lessons, Kiyosaki encourages readers to invest in their financial education, take calculated risks, and build passive income streams to create wealth.
